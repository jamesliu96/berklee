Steps for Burning a Data CD

Before you begin, make sure that your computer is set up to mount blank CDs on the desktop. To confirm this, open System Preferences>CDs and DVDs. Choose "When you insert a blank CD: Open Finder" as displayed below.

  1. Insert a blank CDR into your drive. You'll see it appear on your desktop as Untitled CD.
  2. Name the CD.
  3. Drag the folders and files that you want to write to the CD. This may take a few minutes while the data is being copied to a disk image. The CD is not being written at this point!
  4. Once you have everything arranged in the CD, choose Burn Disc. from the file menu.

Once the data has been written, the CD is locked and no further changes can be made.

For more information you can refer to the OSX online tutorial.
