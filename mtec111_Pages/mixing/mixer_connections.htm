Mixing Board Connections
Mackie 1604VLZ 
 

In the Mackie 1604 mixing console all the connections are made on the back panel.

  • Balanced XLR Microphone inputs.
  • Balanced or unbalanced, 1/4 inch line level inputs for synths or samplers.
  • Unbalanced, 1/4 inch TRS send and return channel inserts.
  • Balanced or unbalanced, 1/4 inch main mix outputs (stereo and mono)
  • Unbalanced, stereo main mix insert, 1/4 inch TRS send and return.
  • Unbalanced, RCA tape deck input and output
  • Balanced or unbalanced, 1/4 inch control room monitor outputs.
  • Balanced or unbalanced, 1/4 inch submix outputs.
  • Balanced or unbalanced 1/4 inch auxiliary sends and returns. 6 mono sends, 4 stereo or 8 mono returns.
  • Balanced or unbalanced, 1/4 inch channel outputs. (Channels 1-8)
