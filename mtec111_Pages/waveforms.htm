Periodic Waveforms

Sound in nature is a complex phenomenon. Often times to illustrate underlying concepts we can use simple models. Simple Periodic Waveforms illustrate patterns of vibrations which exist in a pitched sound. The following illustrates the relationship between timbre (tone color), wave shape and partials. What effect does the number and level of partials have on the sound?
